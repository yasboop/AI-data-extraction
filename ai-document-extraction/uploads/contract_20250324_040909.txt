TERMS AND CONDITIONS
===========================================================
1. SERVICES
   The Service Provider agrees to provide cloud hosting and management
   services as detailed in Appendix A, including but not limited to
   server maintenance, data backup, and security monitoring.

2. TERM
   This Agreement commences on the Effective Date (April 1, 2024) and
   continues until the Expiration Date (March 31, 2025), unless
   terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
   3.1 Service Fee: 2,500 per quarter
   3.2 Payment Schedule: Invoiced quarterly in advance
   3.3 Payment Due: Within 30 days of invoice date
   3.4 Late Payment: Subject to 1.5% monthly interest on outstanding amounts

4. RENEWAL CLAUSE
   This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year
   periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal
   at least sixty (60) days prior to the Expiration Date.

5. KEY LEGAL OBLIGATIONS
   5.1 Service Provider Obligations:
       - Maintain 99.9% uptime for all services
       - Implement industry-standard security protocols
       - Provide 24/7 technical support with 2-hour response time
       - Perform weekly data backups with 30-day retention
   5.2 Client Obligations:
       - Pay all fees when due
       - Designate a primary technical contact person
       - Comply with acceptable use policies
       - Report any security incidents within 24 hours

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary
   information for a period of five (5) years beyond the termination
   of this Agreement.

7. TERMINATION
   Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days
   written notice for material breach that remains uncured.
